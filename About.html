  • The NSBE @ UCR chapter is open to anyone and everyone. Whether you're an engineer or not, as long as you have an interest in any of the various fields of engineering, we welcome you to join us in our support of the NSBE mission. We strive to encourage the younger generation to pursue their education in the STEM field, and we show the opportunities and benefits of doing so. We have events throughout the quarter, tailored to our members and their needs. This would include resume workshops, having guest speakers to talk about industry, regional and national conferences where employers come to you and offer jobs and internships. As well, many of our past members take an active role in making sure our current members are well taken care of.
